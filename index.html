Hello!

I am Mousumi Nath, a PhD researcher in computational chemistry at IISER Kolkata. My research focuses on excited-state photophysics, singlet fission, molecular spintronics, small molecule activation, and machine learning for materials discovery.

🌈 About Me

I work at the interface of quantum chemistry, electronic structure theory, and machine learning to understand and design functional molecular systems for optoelectronic and energy applications.
